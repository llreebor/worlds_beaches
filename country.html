THE PERFECT BEACH DAY

ENJOY CURAÇAO PERFECTION

No matter where you travel, wind, rough seas and clouds ruin more beach days than anything else. Curaçao is no different. Follow these tips to hit the beach the way it looks in postcards:

  • Curaçao’s beaches are excellent year-round, but busier in the high season of December-April.

  • The summer monthS typically experience more cloudy days than you’d expect.

  • The wind almost always comes from the east, and the best beaches are on the west coast.

  • For optimal conditions, look for sunny days with little wind that is coming from the east.
